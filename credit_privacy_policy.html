Privacy Policy

Ilya Makarevich ("I", "me", or "my") operates the Credit Tracker mobile application (the "Service").

Credit Tracker does not collect, store, or use any personal information from its users. This application respects your privacy and operates entirely on your device without transmitting any data to external servers.

Information Collection

Credit Tracker is designed with privacy as a core principle. The application:

  • Does not require any personal information to use
  • Does not collect usage statistics or analytics
  • Does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Does not access your device's contacts, photos, or other personal data
  • Does not share any information with third parties

Payment Data and Purchase History

The App uses RevenueCat to manage trial versions and paid subscriptions. RevenueCat's Privacy Policy can be found here.

When you make in-app purchases, RevenueCat processes your payment information to manage your subscription. This includes:

  • Purchase history and subscription status
  • Information necessary to process payments (handled by the app store)
  • Device information related to subscription management

Credit Tracker itself does not have access to your payment details. All payment processing is handled securely through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store, following their respective privacy policies and security standards.

Data Storage

All data you enter into Credit Tracker (such as credit information, payment history, or notes) is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not backed up to any cloud services unless you explicitly use your device's backup system (like iCloud or Google Drive backups).

Security

Since Credit Tracker does not transmit or store your data on external servers, your information remains secure on your personal device. We recommend using your device's security features (like passcodes, Face ID, or Touch ID) to protect your data.

Third-Party Links

The Service may contain links to other sites. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. Note that these external sites are not operated by me. Therefore, I strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of these websites. I have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

Children's Privacy

My Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. I will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
